Guess Ranking

About the Algorithm

The word ranking algorithms used in this tool are based on Grant Sanderson's excellent Wordle solver video from 3Blue1Brown.

The solver uses information theory to calculate the expected information gain from each possible guess, ranking words by their ability to narrow down the solution space efficiently.

Key concepts:

  • Entropy: Measures uncertainty in the remaining possible answers
  • Information Gain: How much a guess reduces that uncertainty
  • Expected Score: Predicted number of guesses needed if this word is chosen

Watch the video for a beautiful visual explanation of how these concepts apply to word guessing games.
